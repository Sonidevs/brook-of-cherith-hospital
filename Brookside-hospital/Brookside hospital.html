About Brook of Cherith Medical Centre

Brook of Cherith Medical Centre is a trusted private healthcare facility in Nyakarianga, Nyandarua County. We provide high-quality, affordable, and accessible medical services to individuals and families.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services that improve the health and well-being of our community.

Our Vision

To be a leading healthcare provider in Nyandarua County known for excellence, innovation, and compassionate care.

Our Core Values

  • ✔ Compassion and care for all patients
  • ✔ Professionalism and integrity
  • ✔ Excellence in service delivery
  • ✔ Respect for human life
  • ✔ Teamwork and accountability

Services

Outpatient Services

Consultation, diagnosis, and treatment for patients who do not require overnight admission.

Emergency & Accident Care

24/7 emergency services equipped to handle accidents, trauma, and urgent medical conditions.

Inpatient Services

Comfortable hospital rooms and personalized care for patients who need to stay for monitoring or recovery.

Maternity & Child Care

Comprehensive prenatal, delivery, postnatal, and child healthcare services.

Laboratory Services

Modern diagnostic tests and laboratory services including blood tests, urine analysis, and screening services.

Pharmacy

Fully stocked pharmacy providing prescription medications and health consultations.

Radiology & Imaging

Advanced imaging services including X-rays, ultrasound, and other diagnostic imaging techniques.

Chronic Care & Specialized Clinics

Management of chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease through specialized clinics.
